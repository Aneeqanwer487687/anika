Triple Chocolate Mousse Cake

Bakery Style – Rich & Creamy

Time Required

  • Preparation: 45 minutes
  • Baking: 25 minutes
  • Chilling: 6–8 hours

Chocolate Sponge Ingredients

  • ½ cup Maida
  • ¼ cup Cocoa Powder
  • ½ cup Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • ¼ cup Oil
  • ¼ cup Milk
  • ½ tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Vanilla Essence

Sponge Method

  1. Oven 170°C preheat
  2. Eggs & sugar 3–4 min beat
  3. Oil, milk & vanilla add
  4. Dry ingredients fold
  5. 20–25 min bake

Dark Chocolate Mousse

  • 120g Dark Chocolate
  • 1 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Gelatin

Milk Chocolate Mousse

  • 120g Milk Chocolate
  • 1 cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Gelatin

Glossy Chocolate Ganache

  • 100g Dark Chocolate
  • ½ cup Fresh Cream
  • 1 tbsp Butter
